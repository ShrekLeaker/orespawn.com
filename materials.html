A - L
Amethysts can be found, rarely, deep underground. You can make some awesome stuff with this purple beauty!
They make some pretty cool Amethyst Tools & Amethyst Sword, and some of the best armor you can get: Amethyst Armor!!! 
Amethyst blocks are seriously purple!
This is what you get when you punch a tree in the Crystal Dimension. You need these!
Crafted from Crystal Tree Logs. You did punch a few trees, didn't you?
Craft a bunch of these for tools, weapons, and arrows. Use the Crystal Planks.
Similar to coal, but lasts much longer. Tricky stuff to mine, as it tends to explode and catch things on fire. Be careful!
Generally found deep down underneath the dungeon maze in the Crystal Dimension. Use these to craft a Fairy Sword!
Generally found deep down underneath the dungeon maze in the Crystal Dimension. Use these to craft a Rat Sword, one of my favorite weapons! Careful though, because they do tend to spawn a few rats while being mined...
Oddly enough, this is actually PLANTABLE. Yes. Just place it on a grass block and watch it grow. When it is fully grown (3 high and 9 total leaf blocks) it will be a Duplicator Tree and will then begin duplicating any block placed around its base!!! Your own personal zerox machine...
Doesn't do much, just looks kind of interesting.
Eye-of-Ender blocks can be used to summon a Cephadrome. Place an Eye-of-Ender block on the ground, then top it off with an Extreme Torch. Poof! 
Similar to stone in the home world. Use for Kyanite tools and weapons, but not armor. Also makes a nice Crystal Furnace, which is much faster and more fun to watch than a regular stone furnace! Pronounced: ki-an-ite

Wicked cool stuff! Slipperier than snot on a doorknob! Try walking on it. Run into it and get bounced off. What would happen if you made a floor and walls of the stuff, and then put a Zombie or a few Enderman in it? I wonder... Lava Foam can only be found/mined near lots and lots of lava... somewhere like, the Nether!!!
M - Z
Congratulations! You killed Mobzilla! I don't know why you wouldn't make armor, but now you can use the Mobzilla scales to make a beautiful Mobzilla block! It's just pretty.
Molenoid Dirt is produced from the Molenoid! If you come across some, be on the look out. No one likes to get suffocated. This dirt is self cleaning which means it deletes itself. It also give you slowness while walking on it!
Go mining and collect these to make Pink Tourmaline Ingots. Pretty tough and durable stuff. Pronounced: tour-ma-leen.
Makes the second-best armor in the Crystal Dimension. Your Girlfriend loves this stuff. She will happily wear Pink Tourmaline Armor!
For ease of transport, or for building, a nice pink block.
Only a seriously manly miner can mine rubies! Why? Because they only appear directly under a lava pool! Yep. Mine that Ruby Ore block, and the lava lets loose... Good luck with that!
Rubies can be used to make ruby tools and sword. You can also make some awesome Ruby Armor. But the best reason to go mining for rubies is: a ThunderStaff!
A bright red block, great for haunted houses or just some colorful decoration!
Salt blocks kill ants. Great for keeping ants out of places you don't want them! Place a few Salt blocks in front of your door. Double-wide does the trick. Sometimes ants will cross just one block.

Or you can smelt this to make some salt for your popcorn! Yum!
Can't do much with Sky Tree logs except make planks with them. Well, I'm lying.... Build something out of them, then break one. What happens? Cool, eh? Very useful for trolling 'friends'.

This cubic little guy will transport any player that steps on it to a random position up to about 16 blocks away. Great for confusing people! Looks almost identical to that other dark purple stuff that looks just like this...
This is what you want to go mining down deep for in the Crystal Dimension! Very delicate in this form, and not every block will drop an item! But after you've smelted it, this stuff is great for armor, tools and weapons!
The best stuff in the Crystal Dimension. This is for you, although it still looks pretty good on your Girlfriend too! Make some Tigers Eye Armor and some tigers eye tools & weapons!
A block, for ease of transport, or building with if you've managed to find enough.
Oh yeah! Get excited! You're on your way to crafting something Ultimate! This is what you've been looking for! Smelt these to get Titanium Nuggets.
Titanium Nuggets are smelted from Titanium Ore. You'll needs lots of them to have earned the Ultimate Sword!
Oh yeah! Now you can craft an Ultimate weapon, tools, or Ultimate Armor!! Well, assuming you have Uranium too...
For the person who just has too much Titanium to know what do do with it. This block actually sparkles!
