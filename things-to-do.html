Things to do!
Things you should (or maybe shouldn't) do!

Fighting

  • Survive a night in the Village Dimension. Can you save the Village?
  • Get yourself a Girlfriend, and give her an Ultimate Sword.
  • Defeat a few of the major mobs: Water Dragon, Basilisc, Emperor Scorpion, Alien's little brother, Robo-Warrior, Kyuubi...
  • Take on an Emperor Scorpion with a sword. Yeah. No Ultimate Bow. No cheating!!!
  • Ride a Dragon and kill MOTHRA!
  • Ride a Cephadrome and kill a Kraken!
  • Ride a Cephadrome at night, really slow, really low. It's like riding a mob-mower!
  • Get over your fear of bees and go kill one! Yeah, that one. The BIG one.
  • Kill a Jumpy Bug with a sword!
  • Make a Kraken Repellent, and go nuts on the Attack Squid!
  • Make a few Creeper Repellents and protect your property.
  • Kill a Nightmare. Lol!
  • Launch a creeper sky high! :)
  • Fight Mobzilla. Or just watch him as he destroys the entire Village Dimension...
  • Play Whack-A-Mole with the worms!
  • Take on Vortex, and try not to get thrown sky high!
  • Defeat The King! Adopt The Prince!
  • Fight your way through the Challenge Dungeon and all the others!
  • Kill the Queen and make Queen Scale Armor. Lol! Just kidding...

Just for Fun

  • Plant a garden with moth plants and some torches in the Utopia Dimension. Kick your feet up and relax a bit.
  • Make a dance floor under an Experience tree and stay up late dancing with your Girlfriend.
  • Tame a Hydrolisc.
  • Go fishing in the Nether with an Ultimate Fishing Rod.
  • Build a Home Base in a Magic Apple Tree in the Utopia Dimension, take a moment to watch the Fireflies at night. They're gorgeous!
  • Plant a Cherry and Peach Orchard!
  • Try the Instant Garden. Great time-saver.
  • Build an animal farm!
  • Heck, build a fish farm too!
  • Feed your pet Dragon a snowball. Coal to change him back.
  • Ride your pet Dragon, and press 'A' or 'D'. Nifty, eh?
  • Try out a Chainsaw on a tree!
  • Throw a rock at some glass!
  • Try skipping a rock on water!
  • Kiss a Frog!

Adventure

  • Find a Basilisc maze, and get through it without cheating.
  • Visit each of the four Ant Dimensions (Utopia, Mining, Village, & Danger).
  • Go mining in the Mining Dimension.
  • Find at least one of each of the Ancient Dried Ores!
  • Ride a Hoverboard!
  • Find a Ruby Bird Dungeon - only in the Utopia Dimension.
  • Did you find at least two of the six new dungeons?
  • Ride an Ostrich!
  • Build yourself a home in the Crystal Dimension. Stay awake and watch the chaos!
  • Try your hand at mining in the Danger Dimension!
  • Go on a quest to get armor for your girlfriend!
  • Ride a Giant Spider Robot!!!!!

Crafting, Building, & Planting

  • Plant an Apple Tree.
  • Chop down a Sky Tree.
  • Plant a Magic Apple, or two, or three (OMG! No! Don't!).
  • Find a Duplicator Tree, and duplicate a diamond block!
  • Plant an Experience Tree.
  • Make yourself a complete set of Ultimate Armor and Ultimate tools and weapons.
  • Find something fun to do with Lava Foam.
  • Try using a Miner's Dream in the Mining Dimension.
  • Make a bag of Popcorn.
  • Make a Corn Dog!
  • Mine some rubies and make yourself a Thunderstaff!
  • Make a Cephadrome spawner using an Eye of Ender Block.
  • Make some Bacon!
  • Try some Butter Candy!
  • Build a Home Base in a Magic Apple Tree in the Utopia Dimension.
  • BUILD A ZOO!!!!! Don't forget to use the ZooKeeper Items on your critters!
  • Go Sifting in dirt, grass, sand, gravel, and water!
  • Make a Nightmare Sword!
  • Make a pizza, a BLT, and a salad. Serve lunch to your friends!
  • Make a Big Bertha Sword! Check out the range!
  • Make a Rat Sword!
  • Craft an Attitude Adjuster. Adjust someone's attitude!
  • Try a Crabby Patty!
