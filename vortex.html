Vortex

Look out! Vortex is a Boss! She like to swirl mobs up into the air and throw them to the ground! She's leading the takeover of the Crystal Dimension and likes to haul Fairies off to be her slaves. Can you stop her?

Sure. The first couples times you encounter her, you may get lucky. But when you find yourself all of a sudden about a mile high up in the air... Don't say I didn't warn you!

An eye for an eye! Use her eye to make Big Bertha!

Special Skills
Throwing mobs up in the air
Enslaving Fairies
Mesmerizing male players with those incredible eyes!

How to Kill
Any way you can.
