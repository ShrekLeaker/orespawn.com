Ancient Dried OreSpawn Eggs
This is how the mod started and got its name! 

Re-hydrate an Ancient Dried OreSpawn Egg by adding a bucket of water to it on the crafting table. Ancient Dried Ore Spawn Eggs can be found only at shallow depths and are very, very common in the Mining Dimension! There is one for most every creature in this mod and most from the vanilla game. They are actually fun to collect, and to spawn upon an unsuspecting friend! :)  Can you find them all?
